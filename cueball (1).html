HOW IT WORKS

From finding a partner to collecting your winnings — the full flow in four steps.

01
Find a Partner or Venue
Use GPS to find players nearby and open tables within 3–4 km. Filter by tier so you always play fair competition.
02
Set the Entry Fee
Both players put in a match entry fee — minimum ₹50, custom amount. Winner takes all (90%). CueBall keeps a 10% platform fee.
03
QR Check-In & Play
Scan the venue QR on arrival. Play your Best of 3 or Best of 5 — venue host monitors the match for fair play.
04
Result Confirmed & Ranked
Venue host confirms the result. ELO updates instantly based on win/loss, streak, and games per match. Leaderboard refreshed.
